Government Engineering College Jamui

Government Engineering College (GEC), Jamui, is a government engineering college in Bihar, established in 2018 under the Department of Science and Technology. It was initially affiliated with Aryabhatta Knowledge University (2018-2022) and is now affiliated with Bihar Engineering University (since March 2023). Approved by AICTE, the college offers seven branches in the Bachelor of Technology program, with an intake of 60 students per branch.
